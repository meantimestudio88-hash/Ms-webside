A studio for measured spaces

Meantime is an interior design studio working across residential and small-scale commercial projects. Each commission is treated as a study in light, proportion and tactility rather than a one-size-fits-all solution.

We favour quiet gestures, considered details and long-term relationships with clients, collaborators and makers. The work is driven by research and dialogue — every plan, material and junction is carefully tested before it is built.

Studio

Meantime Studio Pte Ltd
Independent interior design practice based in Outram, Singapore.

Focus

Homes, mixed-use spaces and intimate hospitality projects with a restrained, contemporary tone.

Process

Research, drawing, mock-ups and on-site prototyping to ensure each project feels specific rather than generic.

Approach

01

Research & Dialogue

We begin with close observation of how you live and work, mapping routines, adjacencies and existing light.

02

Spatial Framework

Plans, sections and physical mock-ups test proportion, circulation and views before finishes are introduced.

03

Material & Detail

A restrained palette of materials and joinery details is developed to give each project a calm, coherent character.

04

On-site Stewardship

We stay closely involved on site, working with contractors and makers to protect the intent of the design.
